About Us

We are an upstart company that is committed to reducing electronic waste. We offer free electronic pickups of electronic waste in the Metro Vancouver area.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote sustainability and reduce electronic waste through various initiatives.

Reuse & Recycle

We aim to reuse and recycle as much electronic waste as possible. This keeps toxic chemicals out of landfills and reduces the environmental impact of e-waste.

Bridge the Digital Divide

We hope to that our services will help bridge the digital divide by providing access to technology for underserved communities.

Promote Free and Open Source Software

We believe that open-source software helps users take control of their technology and gain more privacy and security than from proprietary alternatives.
